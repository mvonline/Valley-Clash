Valley Clash

Fan tribute — stylized vector likeness, original art only. Masters of the Valley & beyond. Not affiliated with DreamWorks. Arena rendered with PixiJS (2D canvas composited to GPU).

Controls

Player 1: AD move · W jump · S hold block · J punch · K kick

Player 2: arrows move · ↑ jump · ↓ block · V punch · B kick (numpad 4/5 also)

Combos

Each master has two unique chains. Enter the sequence on the ground while idle — the final button triggers their signature move (heavy damage, cinematic flair). Watch the character select panel for exact inputs (P1 uses J/K; P2 uses V/B).

Match

First to win two rounds. Reduced damage while blocking. Each round starts with a 3-2-1 countdown, then FIGHT!

Sound · Procedural SFX and looping background music (Web Audio — calmer theme on menus, faster theme in the arena). Tap / click / press a key if audio is silent (autoplay rules).
